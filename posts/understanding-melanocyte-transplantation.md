Non-cultured melanocyte-keratinocyte transplant (NCMT) is a revolutionary surgical technique used to treat stable vitiligo. By harvesting healthy skin cells from a donor area, separating the melanocytes, and transplanting them onto depigmented areas, we can restore natural pigmentation with high success rates.
